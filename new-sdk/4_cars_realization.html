This section explains the inner workings and creation of vehicles in Crashday.


Every car needs it's folder in 4 essential places: cars, models, shop, and textures folders. If a one of these is missing, then they won't work ingame, or crash the game.

The one in the 'cars' folder is the heart of your car, it contains the main file, where the performance and basic things are set.

The one in the 'models' and 'textures' folder contains the model and textures respectively.

The one in the 'shop' folder makes the car and it's tuning parts appear in the game.

You can download other, or share your own cars using the Steam Workshop.

If created a car correctly, it should appear in the game's quick play or career mode.
